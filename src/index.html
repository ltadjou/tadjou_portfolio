Building A Corporate Corpus For Threads Constitution

Here we built a corporate corpus based on emails data. We specifically address the process of collecting, preprocessing and pseudo-anonymizing emails in order to meet legal, personal, acceptability and social constraints. This comprises the use of models for Named Entity Recognition (NER), rule-based systems and their evaluation. In the upcoming sections, we will present research works on corpus building and constraints related to it.
To Collect data, in a more practical way and because Microsoft Outlook is the application used in the company for emails exchanges, we developed a C# Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF) application based on Microsoft Office Interface that allows us to interact with Outlook application and also with backup files from Outlook.

Dialogue Acts Identification & Utterances Pairing

Here I build corpora that I then use to train models for two tasks as part of the resolution of the problem of my PhD which is: the constitution of sub-threads of email conversations.

  • Used corpora :
    - Meeting Recorder Dialogue Act Corpus (MRDA)
    - BC3: Corpus of emails
  • Tasks :

    1- Dialogue Acts Identification: Train models for the classification of statements into acts of dialogue.
    2- Utterances Pairing: : Train model to pair Utterances from conversations in a transversal manner
